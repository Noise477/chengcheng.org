About Me

I am a final-year Bachelor of Science student majoring in Computer Science and Information & Technology Management at the University of Auckland. My interests include educational technology and automated assessment systems.

I have developed a customizable Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) tool that is now actively used across multiple courses at the University of Auckland.

I'm also a Teaching Assistant for COMPSCI 335 – Web Programming and Distributed Services.

My recent papers were accepted by IEEE FIE 2025 and TALE 2025, where I explored customizable OMR technologies and secure online question generation.
