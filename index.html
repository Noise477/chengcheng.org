About Me

I recently graduated from the University of Auckland with a Bachelor of Science, majoring in Computer Science and Information Technology Management. I am interested in building practical software systems that improve efficiency and solve real-world problems.

I developed a customizable Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) system that is actively used across multiple courses at the University of Auckland, helping reduce marking time and improve data processing efficiency.

I am currently working as a Research Assistant, contributing to projects in educational technology, with a focus on secure assessment.

I previously worked as a Teaching Assistant for COMPSCI 335 – Web Programming and Distributed Services, supporting students with backend development and distributed system concepts.

I also have research experience with published papers at IEEE FIE, IEEE TALE, and EDUNINE.
